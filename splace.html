SKEYP

SKEYP is a phone-based application exploring the seams of geolocation by leaving behind soundtraces for others to dicover. When reaching the locations of the sound traces they will play automatically creating a context awareness for the user by placing a specific sound to a specific place.
SKEYP is a prototype and more of a hobby project than an actual commercial idea.
