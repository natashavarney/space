Nordea finans check out flow

Nordea Finans wanted to incorporate a payment method and an interactive loan calculator into Suzuki, Mazda and Volvos check out flow. I created a simple mockup of the checkout process and user journey in Figma that had to be simple and elegant portraying just enough information for the user to find the tool useful without confusing them in an already confusion process of buying a car online.
We found that people was eager to get a profesional assessment before picing out their dream. This way they would not end up being dissapointed when finding out that they could not afford it.
The Suzuki and Mazda mockups are not portrayed but has similar features corresponding to their visual identity and common user flow.
